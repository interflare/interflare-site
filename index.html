Exactly what you need from a server

Custom private worlds

As an alternative to hosting your own server for a large project, you can rent a private world for you and your builders.

WorldEdit available

Players that have been online for a certain amount of time are granted access to a limited version of WorldEdit.

Web world maps

An online map can be extremely useful when working on projects with a large footprint, which is why we provide an easy-to-use web map to assist you.

Hardened protection

To prevent griefing in the public worlds, we log everything through CoreProtect, and offer self-service and managed WorldGuard.

Lightning fast connection

We use top of the line cloud services with a redundant 40gbps network to ensure that our servers are fast and responsive.

Infallible uptime

Our continous upgrade system allows our servers to always be online and available, whenever you want to play.
